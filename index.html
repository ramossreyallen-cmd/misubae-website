About Us

Misubae was born from a love for Japanese cuisine and Filipino creativity. Our founders, passionate about bringing affordable, flavorful, and fresh food to everyone, created a unique fusion that celebrates both cultures.

Our mission is simple: to serve affordable, flavorful, and fresh musubi that brings joy to every bite.

Each musubi is carefully prepared with the freshest ingredients, ensuring every bite is a memorable experience. From our kitchen to your hands, we put love into every wrap.

We believe that great food should be accessible to all, which is why we've dedicated ourselves to crafting the perfect musubi - a delightful combination of Japanese tradition and Filipino flair.
